Traditional Mexican Cuisine.

These traditional style dishes represent the Mexican culture that has been carried on for years. Below are ten examples of popular and traditional dishes found in Mexico:

  • Chilaquiles
  • Pozole
  • Tacos al pastor
  • Tostadas
  • Elote
  • Chiles en nogada
  • Enchiladas
  • Mole
  • Guacamole
  • Tamales
  • Horchata

    • All of these dishes are well-known within the Mexican community and world-wide!
