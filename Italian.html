Italian Dishes

By: Cameron Billingsley

These are some of the most popular Italian food dishes. Below is a list and pictures of each of the dishes and provides a brief background on the items:




Arancini- This dish is rice balls filled with ragu, tomato sauce, peas, and mozzarella.


Lasagne- Lasagne is a very popolar dish that is beleived to come from Naples, it is created with layers of lasagna pasta and chesse, meat, vegtables, and sauces.


Prosciutto- This food item is dry-cured ham that is served uncooked and cut into thin slices. It is most often served with pasta or cheese slices.


Gelato- Gelato is a very popular Italian style ice cream. It is a frozen dessert made with milk and sugar and generally has a lower in fat than other frozen icecreams.


Tiramisu- This dish is another dessert whos ingrideints include coffee, eggs, sugar, cocoa, and mascarpone cheese.

While there are many more popular Italian dishes, these are just a few that I like the most, and are a little different than basic food dishes you may think of.
