Japanese Cuisine

Hello, have you ever tried Japanese food? If not there are plenty of meals that are great and are out there for you to explore with different amazing flavors! Here are some highlights of popular Japanese food.



Sushi

This is well known through out the world and is the most famous. It is a great meal to have on a first date.

Soba

Reports says that Soba is a noodle dish that has been eaten in Japan for centuries. It can be a delicious and healthy option anytime of the year!

Sashimi

This is interesting! Centuries before Japanese people were eating sushi, they first enjoyed raw fish without rice called sashimi.
